[ Apple Pie ]

Ingredients

  • Pie Dough: 230g All-purpose flour, 120g Cold butter, 50g Cold water, 2g Salt
  • Apple Filling: 2-3 Apples, 70g Sugar, 1 tablespoon Lemon juice, 1 teaspoon Cinnamon powder
  • Others: 1 Egg (for brushing)

Steps

  1. Prepare the pie dough: Mix flour and salt, then cut in cold butter until crumbly. Add cold water and knead into a dough. Wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the apple filling: Slice apples (0.5cm thick), cook with sugar and lemon juice over medium heat until caramelized. Add cinnamon powder and cool.
  3. Roll the chilled dough to 0.3cm thickness. Line a tart pan and prick the base with a fork.
  4. Fill the tart with the prepared apple filling.
  5. Use remaining dough to create lattice strips and place on top of the filling. Brush with beaten egg.
  6. Bake at 190°C for 30 minutes until golden brown. Let it cool and serve.
