[ Apple Jam ]

Ingredients

  • 5 Apples
  • 500g Sugar
  • 4 tablespoons Lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon Cinnamon powder

Steps

  1. Sterilize a glass jar by boiling it in water for 3-5 minutes. Let it dry completely.
  2. Wash the apples thoroughly.
  3. Remove the seeds and chop the apples into desired sizes.
  4. In a pot, add 1050g of chopped apples and 500g of sugar. Cover the pot and heat on low for 20 minutes.
  5. Once the sugar starts to melt, increase to medium heat and stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Add 4 tablespoons of lemon juice and 1/2 tablespoon of cinnamon powder. Adjust quantities to taste.
  7. Cook until the apples reach a jam-like consistency, stirring occasionally (about 35 minutes).
  8. Once the jam is ready, pour it into the sterilized jar while hot. Seal the jar and let it cool upside down for a vacuum seal.
  9. Enjoy the apple jam on warm toast or as a sweet treat!
