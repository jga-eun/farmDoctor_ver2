[ Pear Jam ]

Ingredients

  • 4 Pears
  • Sugar (equal weight as pears)
  • 4 tablespoons Lemon juice (or Lime juice)

Steps

  1. Dice 4 pears into small pieces or puree them for a smoother texture.
  2. Add sugar (same weight as pears) to a large pot with the pears.
  3. Mix well and cook on medium heat with a lid on for about 20 minutes.
  4. Remove the lid and continue cooking, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
  5. When the mixture thickens to a light brown color, add 4 tablespoons of lemon juice.
  6. Cook until the jam reaches the desired consistency and remove from heat.
  7. Cool the jam and store it in a sterilized container. Refrigerate for freshness.

Tip: Pear jam pairs well with bread, yogurt, or as a sweetener in meat dishes.
