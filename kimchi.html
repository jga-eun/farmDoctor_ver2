[ Kimchi ]

Ingredients

  • 1 Bunch of Young Cabbage (400g)
  • 900g Napa Cabbage
  • 500g Radish
  • 1 Optional Onion
  • 1 Green Onion or Leek
  • 4 Tbsp Coarse Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Red Pepper Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Red Pepper Powder (extra for color)
  • For Seasoning:
  • 3 Tbsp Cold Rice
  • 1/3 Cup Fish Sauce
  • 1/2 Tbsp Salted Shrimp
  • 3–4 Tbsp Plum Extract
  • 1.5 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 Tsp Ginger Syrup
  • 1/4 Apple
  • 1/2 Onion

Steps

  1. Separate and trim young cabbage leaves; slice napa cabbage and radish into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Rinse thoroughly and sprinkle with coarse salt. Let it sit for 2 hours, turning occasionally.
  3. Rinse the salted vegetables several times and drain well.
  4. Lightly mix the vegetables with red pepper powder.
  5. Blend the seasoning ingredients (cold rice, fish sauce, shrimp, plum extract, garlic, ginger, apple, and onion) in a blender.
  6. Mix the blended seasoning with the vegetables evenly.
  7. Add extra onion slices or green onion for garnish and flavor.
  8. Serve immediately as fresh kimchi (optional: sprinkle sesame seeds and sesame oil), or store in an airtight container.
  9. Let it ferment at room temperature for a day before moving to the fridge for storage.
