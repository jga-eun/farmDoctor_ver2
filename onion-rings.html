[ Onion Rings ]

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the onions into rings.
  2. Coat each ring with flour, dip in beaten egg, and coat with bread crumbs.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the rings until golden brown.
  4. Drain on a paper towel, add salt and pepper, and serve hot.
