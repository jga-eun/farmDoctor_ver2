[ Lettuce Side Dish ]

Ingredients

  • Vegetables: 1 bag Lettuce, 1/2 Onion
  • Seasoning: 1T Red pepper powder, 1T Soy sauce, 1T Fish sauce, 1T Sugar, 1T Minced garlic, 1T Chopped green onion, 1T Sesame oil, 1T Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Wash the lettuce thoroughly and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Slice the onion thinly. You can also add other vegetables like carrots if desired.
  3. Mix all seasoning ingredients except sesame oil and sesame seeds in a bowl.
  4. Add the seasoning to the lettuce and onion gradually, adjusting to your taste.
  5. Add sesame oil and sesame seeds, then gently toss to combine. Avoid overmixing to prevent the lettuce from wilting.
  6. Serve immediately as a refreshing side dish!
