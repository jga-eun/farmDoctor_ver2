[ Green Onion Pancake ]

Ingredients

  • 200g Green Onions
  • 1 Squid (cleaned and sliced)
  • 2 Handfuls of Shrimp
  • 2 Eggs
  • For Batter:
  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Tempura Flour
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 Tbsp Cornstarch
  • For Dipping Sauce:
  • 3 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 Tbsp Minced Green Onion
  • 1/2 Tbsp Sesame Seeds
  • 1/2 Tbsp Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Wash and trim green onions, then cut them in half.
  2. Prepare the batter by mixing flour, tempura flour, water, and cornstarch.
  3. Blanch squid and shrimp in boiling water for 1 minute, then set aside.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, coat green onions with the batter.
  5. Heat a generous amount of oil in a pan and spread the green onions evenly on it.
  6. Top with squid and shrimp, then pour the remaining batter and beaten eggs over the top.
  7. Cook over medium heat until the bottom is golden and crispy. Flip and cook the other side.
  8. Prepare the dipping sauce by combining all the sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  9. Serve the pancake hot with the dipping sauce on the side.

This crispy and savory pancake is perfect for sharing on a rainy day!
