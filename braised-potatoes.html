[ Braised Potatoes ]

Ingredients

  • 500g Potatoes
  • For Seasoning:
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Oligodang (Corn Syrup)
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes thoroughly, peel, and cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, combine water, soy sauce, sugar, corn syrup, minced garlic, cooking oil, and sesame oil to make the braising sauce.
  3. Place the cut potatoes into a pot and pour the braising sauce over them.
  4. Ensure the potatoes are mostly submerged in the sauce.
  5. Cover the pot and bring it to a boil over high heat for 15 minutes.
  6. After 15 minutes, check if the sauce has reduced. Stir gently and cook uncovered for another 5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your flavorful and tender braised potatoes!
